My Story

My name is Erin Aundrea Burnett! I am a junior, at the University of Maryland, majoring in broadcast journalism and minoring in Technology Entrepreneurship. I have wanted to be a broadcast journalist, since the 4th grade. Inspired by shows like the Today Show, and media legends like Savannah Guthrie, Tamron Hall and Lester Holt; I fell in love with journalism's potential to transport and inspire people without them even leaving home. Having a naturally energetic, out-going, creative and curious personality, I believe that journalism is my calling. Specifically, reporting, producing and the business-side of the media industry are the sectors of the industry where my core career-goals lie.

Mission Statement


Aspiring broadcast journalist, interested in world and political news and the intersection between media, technology and its' societal impact. Called to inspire and motivate through storytelling. Studying journalism and technology entrepreneurship at the Merrill School of Journalism at the University of Maryland
